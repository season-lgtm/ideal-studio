What to Do Before & After a Facial

By IDEAL STUDIO · Singapore · Updated 2026-06-25

A little prep and aftercare makes a big difference. Here's how to get the most from every facial.

Before your facial

  • Come with clean skin if you can, or we'll cleanse for you.
  • Pause strong actives (retinol, AHA/BHA) for 2–3 days before extraction-based facials.
  • Tell us about allergies, recent treatments or medication.
  • Avoid waxing or sunburn on the area right before.

After your facial

  • First 24 hours: skip makeup, saunas, intense workouts and direct sun.
  • Keep skin hydrated and always wear SPF the next day.
  • Don't pick at any extraction spots — let them heal.
  • Hold off on strong actives for 1–2 days.

What's normal

Slight redness after extraction is normal and settles within a few hours. If you booked add-ons like Hydra Resurfacing, your skin may look extra glowy — that's the point.

FAQ

Common questions

Can I wear makeup after a facial?

Best to wait 24 hours so pores stay clean and calm. After that, makeup is fine.

Why is my skin red after a facial?

Mild redness after extraction or massage is normal and usually fades within a few hours.
