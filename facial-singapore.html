Facial questions

How often should I get a facial?

For most skin, once every 3–4 weeks keeps results steady. Acne-prone skin may start fortnightly, then move to monthly maintenance.

Which facial is best for acne in Singapore?

Our Deep Clean with full extraction and high-frequency, plus LED therapy to calm breakouts. Read the full guide →

How much does a facial cost?

From $48 (Express Clean) and $68 (Deep Clean), with a $38 first-trial. All nett, no membership or package.

Is there any downtime?

Minimal. You may have slight redness after extraction that settles within hours. We finish with a soothing mask and SPF.
