Classic vs Volume Lashes: How to Choose

By IDEAL STUDIO · Singapore · Updated 2026-06-25

Both make your eyes pop, but they create very different looks. Here's how to choose your lash set.

Classic lashes

One extension is applied to each natural lash for a clean, natural, mascara-like finish. Best if you want subtle enhancement or have healthy natural lashes. (Classic 1D — $55)

Volume lashes

Several ultra-light extensions are fanned onto each natural lash for a fuller, softer, fluffier look. Great for sparse lashes or anyone wanting more drama — and surprisingly lightweight.

Hybrid & styles

A hybrid mixes classic and volume for texture. We also offer styled sets — clover, fairy, manga and wet-look — to suit different eye shapes.

Quick guide

  • Want natural: classic.
  • Want full / sparse lashes: volume.
  • Want texture / trendy: hybrid or a styled set.

Whichever you choose, good aftercare keeps them full — read our lash aftercare guide, or compare with a lash lift.

FAQ

Common questions

Are volume lashes heavier than classic?

No — volume fans use much thinner extensions, so a full volume set can weigh about the same as classic while looking fuller.

Which lasts longer, classic or volume?

Both follow your natural lash cycle (4–6 weeks). Retention depends more on aftercare than on the style.
