Can You Really Shrink Your Pores?

By IDEAL STUDIO · Singapore · Updated 2026-06-25

Honest answer: you can't permanently shrink pores — their size is mostly genetic. But you can make them look noticeably smaller.

Why pores look bigger

  • Excess oil and congestion stretching the pore
  • Blackheads holding pores open — see how to clear blackheads safely
  • Loss of firmness with age
  • Sun damage

What actually minimises them

  • Keep pores clear — regular cleansing, BHA exfoliation and professional extraction stop pores from stretching.
  • Niacinamide helps refine the look of pores over time.
  • Hydrate — plump, hydrated skin makes pores less obvious.
  • Daily SPF — protects the collagen that keeps pores tight.
  • Professional facials — extraction + hydra resurfacing visibly refine texture.

What won't work

Cold water "closing" pores (temporary at best), pore strips (surface only), and harsh scrubbing (makes it worse). Consistency with the basics wins. Book a facial to keep pores clear and refined.

FAQ

Common questions

Can you permanently shrink pores?

No — pore size is mainly genetic. But keeping pores clear, hydrated and protected makes them look significantly smaller.

Do pore strips shrink pores?

No, they only remove surface debris and can irritate skin. Regular cleansing, exfoliation and professional extraction work far better.
