How to Book on WhatsApp at IDEAL STUDIO

By IDEAL STUDIO · Singapore · Updated 2026-06-25

No apps, no forms to wrestle with — you can book your appointment in a couple of WhatsApp messages.

How to book

  1. Tap Book on our website or message us on WhatsApp.
  2. Tell us the service, your preferred date and a rough time.
  3. We confirm an available slot and lock it in.

Browse services first if you like: facials, brows, lashes or nails — all prices are on the price list.

What to include

For PMU and lash sets, a quick note on your goal (or a reference photo) helps us prepare and quote accurately.

Reschedule & reminders

Need to change your time? Just reply in the same chat. You'll also get a friendly reminder before your appointment, so nothing slips.

Why WhatsApp

It's where our clients already are — fast, personal and easy to keep track of. Ready? Book your appointment now.
