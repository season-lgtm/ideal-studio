Face Bojin (拨筋): Benefits for Your Skin

By IDEAL STUDIO · Singapore · Updated 2026-06-25

Face Bojin (脸部拨筋) is a traditional sculpting massage technique that lifts, de-puffs and relieves tension — a natural, no-downtime way to refresh the face.

What it is

Using gentle guided strokes along the face and lymphatic pathways, bojin works the deeper tissue and fascia to release tension and encourage circulation and drainage.

The benefits

  • Lift & definition — sculpts the jaw and cheeks for a more contoured look.
  • De-puffing — supports lymphatic drainage to reduce facial puffiness.
  • Tension relief — eases tight jaw, brow and temple muscles.
  • Glow — improved circulation gives a healthy flush.

Who it suits

Great for puffiness, jaw tension, and anyone wanting a natural lift without injectables. It pairs beautifully in our Signature 3-in-1 (Deep Clean + Eye Treatment + Face Bojin), or as a mini-treatment — see the price list.
