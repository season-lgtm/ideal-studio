How to Get Clear, Glowing "Glass Skin"

By IDEAL STUDIO · Singapore · Updated 2026-06-25

"Glass skin" isn't a filter — it's skin that's clear, deeply hydrated and even-toned, so it reflects light. Here's how to get closer to it.

The foundations

  • Clear congestion first. Glow needs a clean canvas — regular extraction in a deep cleansing facial.
  • Hydrate in layers. Hyaluronic acid + a good moisturiser; dewiness comes from water content.
  • Gentle, regular exfoliation. Smooth texture reflects light better — but don't overdo it (see how often to exfoliate).
  • Even the tone. Niacinamide and vitamin C fade marks and dullness.
  • SPF daily. Protects the glow you build.

The facial that helps most

A hydra resurfacing facial (小气泡) exfoliates and floods skin with hydration in one step — the fastest route to instant, no-downtime glow before an event.

Build it with consistency and regular facials — glow is a habit, not a one-off.

FAQ

Common questions

How do I get glass skin?

Clear congestion, hydrate in layers, exfoliate gently, even your tone with niacinamide/vitamin C, and protect with SPF. Hydra facials speed up the glow.

Is glass skin achievable for oily skin?

Yes — focus on keeping pores clear, hydrating (not over-stripping) and gentle exfoliation. Glass skin is about health, not skin type.
