About Us

Honest beauty, done properly

IDEAL STUDIO is a results-first beauty studio in Singapore — built on transparency, not pressure.

We started IDEAL STUDIO because beauty in Singapore had become about packages, memberships and upselling — not results. We do the opposite: assess honestly, recommend only what helps, and price everything transparently.

What we believe

  • Only what works. If a treatment won't benefit you, we'll tell you to skip it.
  • No membership, no package. Pay per visit at a nett price you can see upfront.
  • Clean & professional. Single-use sterile needles for PMU, hygienic tools, careful hands.

What we do

We specialise in four things and do them well: facials (面部护理), eyebrow embroidery & semi-permanent makeup (纹眉), eyelash extensions (美睫) and gel manicure & nails (美甲). Every price is on our transparent price list.

Why clients choose us

Because there's no catch. You book the service you want, you pay the price we quote, and you leave with a result — not a contract. Read more on our beauty blog, including why "no membership, no package" is better for your skin and wallet.
