Is Eyebrow Embroidery Painful?

By IDEAL STUDIO · Singapore · Updated 2026-06-25

It's the most common question we get. The short answer: most clients feel only mild discomfort, not real pain.

What it feels like

After numbing cream, most people describe a light scratching or tickling sensation rather than pain. It's very manageable — many clients relax or even doze off.

How we keep it comfortable

  • A topical numbing cream is applied before and reapplied during the session.
  • We work in gentle passes and check in with you throughout.
  • Single-use sterile needles, every time.

Does it depend on the area?

Brows are generally the most comfortable. Semi-permanent eyeliner and lip blush can feel slightly more sensitive, but numbing keeps them tolerable too.

After the session

Your brows may feel mildly tender or look a little bold for a few days as they heal. That's normal — see our healing timeline guide. Mild and short-lived discomfort is a small trade for brows that look done every morning.
