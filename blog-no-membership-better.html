Why "No Membership, No Package" Is Better

By IDEAL STUDIO · Singapore · Updated 2026-06-25

Most beauty salons sell packages. We don't — and it's a deliberate choice that's better for you.

The package trap

Prepaid packages tie up your money and create pressure to "use up" sessions, even when your skin doesn't need them. That leads to over-treating, which can sensitise skin and waste your budget.

Better for your skin

Because we have no package, we only book you when a treatment actually helps. If your skin needs a break, we'll say so — see how often you should really get a facial.

Better for your wallet

  • No large upfront commitment.
  • No "use it or lose it" pressure.
  • Transparent, nett prices on our price list — what you see is what you pay.

Honest by design

It also keeps us honest. With nothing to "sell down", our only job is results. That's the whole idea behind IDEAL STUDIO: only what works, at a price you can see.
