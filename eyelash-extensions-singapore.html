FAQ · Lashes

Eyelash extension questions

How long do lash extensions last?

Typically 4–6 weeks, following your natural lash cycle. A refill every 2–3 weeks keeps them full. Aftercare tips →

Classic or volume?

Classic = natural, one-to-one. Volume = fuller and softer with light fans. Compare →

Lash lift or extensions?

A lash lift curls your own lashes (low maintenance); extensions add length and volume. Which is better for you →

Can I wear makeup with lash extensions?

Yes, but avoid oil-based products and waterproof mascara on the lash line, which break down the adhesive.
