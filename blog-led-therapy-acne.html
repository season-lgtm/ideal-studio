Does LED Light Therapy Work for Acne?

By IDEAL STUDIO · Singapore · Updated 2026-06-25

LED therapy is one of the gentlest, no-downtime ways to support clearer, calmer skin — and yes, there's real science behind it.

How it works

  • Blue light targets acne-causing bacteria, helping reduce breakouts.
  • Red light calms inflammation and supports healing and recovery.

It's painless, suitable for most skin types, and has no downtime — which is why we offer it as a facial booster.

What to realistically expect

LED is a support, not a standalone cure. It works best combined with proper cleansing and extraction in a deep cleansing facial, and with consistency over several sessions. Don't expect overnight miracles — expect calmer, clearer skin over time.

Who it suits

Great for active breakouts, post-extraction calming and sensitive skin that can't tolerate harsh actives. Add it to any facial (+$18).
