Eyelash Extensions Aftercare Guide

By IDEAL STUDIO · Singapore · Updated 2026-06-25

Great retention is 80% aftercare. Follow these simple habits and your lash extensions will stay full for weeks.

First 24 hours

Keep lashes dry and avoid steam, saunas and heavy sweat while the adhesive fully cures.

Daily care

  • Cleanse gently with a lash-safe foam every 1–2 days — clean lashes retain better.
  • Brush them with a clean spoolie each morning.
  • Avoid oil-based cleansers, makeup removers and creams near the lash line.
  • Skip waterproof mascara and lash curlers.

What breaks them down

Oil, heat, rubbing your eyes and sleeping face-down are the main culprits. Try a silk pillowcase and sleep on your back if you can.

Refills

You naturally shed lashes daily, so book a refill every 2–3 weeks to keep them looking full. A full new set is best after about 6 weeks. New to lashes? Compare classic vs volume.

FAQ

Common questions

How do I make lash extensions last longer?

Keep them clean and dry early on, avoid oil and heat, brush daily, and book refills every 2–3 weeks.

Can I get lash extensions wet?

Yes, after the first 24 hours. Just avoid oil-based products and rubbing your eyes.
