Skincare Ingredients That Actually Fight Acne

By IDEAL STUDIO · Singapore · Updated 2026-06-25

The acne aisle is overwhelming. Here are the ingredients with real evidence — and how to use them without wrecking your skin.

The proven actives

  • Salicylic acid (BHA): oil-soluble, clears inside the pore. Best for blackheads and congestion.
  • Benzoyl peroxide: kills acne bacteria. Great spot treatment; can dry and bleach fabrics.
  • Niacinamide: calms redness, controls oil, fades marks. Gentle, plays well with everything.
  • Retinoids: speed cell turnover, unclog pores, smooth texture. Start low and slow.
  • Azelaic acid: tackles acne and post-acne marks; good for sensitive skin.

How to use them

  • Introduce one new active at a time, a few nights a week.
  • Don't combine strong actives in the same routine (e.g., BHA + retinoid + benzoyl all at once).
  • Always moisturise and wear SPF — actives make skin sun-sensitive.

FAQ

Common questions

What is the best ingredient for blackheads?

Salicylic acid (BHA) — it's oil-soluble, so it clears congestion inside the pore better than scrubs.

Can I use salicylic acid and retinol together?

Introduce them separately and avoid layering strong actives in one routine, or you risk irritation. Alternate nights and always moisturise.
