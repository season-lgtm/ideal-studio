The Best Facial for Acne-Prone Skin in Singapore

By IDEAL STUDIO · Singapore · Updated 2026-06-25

Acne-prone skin in Singapore's humidity needs deep cleansing and calming — not harsh scrubbing. Here's what an effective acne facial includes.

What a good acne facial does

  • Deep cleanse & skin scan to assess congestion without guessing.
  • Gentle exfoliation & steam to soften pores before extraction.
  • Professional extraction to clear blackheads and whiteheads safely (never pick at home).
  • High-frequency to reduce acne-causing bacteria and calm inflammation.
  • LED therapy (blue/red light) and a soothing mask to settle active breakouts.

Our Deep Clean facial ($68) is built exactly this way, with optional LED therapy (+$18).

What to avoid

Skip aggressive scrubs, over-extraction and "purging on purpose". Harsh treatments break the skin barrier and make acne worse. The goal is clear and calm.

At home

Use a gentle cleanser, a non-comedogenic moisturiser, and SPF every day. Don't pick. For frequency, see how often you should get a facial, and read our facial aftercare guide.

FAQ

Common questions

How many acne facials do I need to see results?

Many clients see improvement after 2–3 sessions, with a deep clean every 2 weeks at first, then monthly maintenance.

Does extraction make acne worse?

Done professionally, extraction clears congestion safely. Picking at home is what spreads bacteria and causes scarring.
