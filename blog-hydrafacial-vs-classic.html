Hydra Facial (小气泡) vs Classic Facial

By IDEAL STUDIO · Singapore · Updated 2026-06-25

Both clean and refresh your skin, but they work differently. Here's how to choose — or combine — them.

Classic facial

A classic facial cleanses, exfoliates, steams, extracts and masks by hand. It's thorough and great for congestion, relaxation and overall skin health — like our Express Clean and Deep Clean facials.

Hydra Resurfacing (小气泡)

Hydra resurfacing uses gentle vacuum-and-infusion to exfoliate, clear pores and flood skin with hydration in one step. It's quick, comfortable and gives an instant glow — offered as a +$28 booster on any facial.

Who needs which?

  • Congested / acne-prone: classic deep clean first, then hydra to refine.
  • Dull, dehydrated, "tired" skin: hydra resurfacing for fast radiance.
  • Event coming up: hydra for glow with no downtime.

Better together

The best results often come from a classic Deep Clean to clear the skin, finished with Hydra Resurfacing to hydrate and brighten. We'll recommend only what your skin scan shows you need.

FAQ

Common questions

Is hydra facial better than a normal facial?

Neither is 'better' — a classic facial deep-cleans and relaxes, hydra resurfacing adds fast hydration and glow. Many clients combine both.

Does hydra resurfacing have downtime?

No real downtime — skin looks glowy immediately, which makes it popular before events.
