Lip Blush 101: A Natural Flush That Lasts

By IDEAL STUDIO · Singapore · Updated 2026-06-25

Lip blush is a soft, semi-permanent tint that enhances your natural lip colour and shape — think "your lips but better", not lipstick.

What it is

Using fine, gentle pigment, lip blush adds a wash of colour and definition. It's perfect if your lips look pale, uneven or lose definition with age. We offer Lip Blush — Monroe Red ($300) and Gradient Crystal Lip ($388).

The healing process

  • Days 1–3: colour looks bright and lips feel a little dry/tight.
  • Days 3–5: light flaking — keep lips balmed, don't pick.
  • Week 1–2: colour softens, then blooms back to a natural flush.

Choosing your colour

We match the tone to your natural lips and skin undertone, so it heals soft and wearable. You can always go a touch deeper at your touch-up.

Who it suits

Great for pale or uneven lips, busy people who want low-maintenance colour, and anyone tired of reapplying lipstick. As with all PMU, we use single-use sterile needles and numbing for comfort — see is it painful.

FAQ

Common questions

How long does lip blush last?

Typically 1–3 years. A refresh keeps the colour vibrant as it naturally softens over time.

Will lip blush look too dark?

No — it looks bold for the first few days, then heals to a soft, natural flush about 30–50% lighter.
