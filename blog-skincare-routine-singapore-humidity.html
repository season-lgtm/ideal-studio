The Best Skincare Routine for Singapore's Humidity

By IDEAL STUDIO · Singapore · Updated 2026-06-25

Singapore's heat and humidity make skin oily on the surface but often dehydrated underneath. The fix isn't stripping your skin — it's balancing it.

Your daily routine

  • Cleanse morning & night with a gentle gel cleanser to remove sweat, sunscreen and excess oil.
  • Hydrate lightweight: a gel or watery moisturiser keeps skin balanced without feeling heavy.
  • Niacinamide helps control oil and minimise the look of pores.
  • SPF every morning — non-negotiable, even indoors near windows.

Weekly

Exfoliate 1–2 times a week with a gentle BHA to keep pores clear in the humidity — but don't overdo it, or you'll trigger more oil.

Where professional facials fit in

Home care maintains; a professional facial resets. A monthly deep clean clears congestion that builds up faster in our climate. See how often you should get a facial for your skin type.

Common humidity mistakes

  • Skipping moisturiser because skin feels oily (this makes oil worse).
  • Over-washing and over-scrubbing.
  • Heavy creams that trap sweat and clog pores.

Get the basics right and your skin stays clearer, calmer and more comfortable all year round.

FAQ

Common questions

Do I need moisturiser if my skin is oily in Singapore?

Yes. Oily skin is often dehydrated underneath. A lightweight gel moisturiser balances skin and can actually reduce excess oil.

How often should I exfoliate in humid weather?

1–2 times a week with a gentle BHA is usually enough. Over-exfoliating triggers more oil and irritation.
