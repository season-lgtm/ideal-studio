How Often Should You Get a Facial?

By IDEAL STUDIO · Singapore · Updated 2026-06-25

The honest answer: it depends on your skin, not on a package someone sold you. Here's how often a facial actually helps in Singapore's humid climate.

The general rule

For most people, a facial every 3 to 4 weeks works well. That matches your skin's natural renewal cycle, so each session builds on the last instead of starting from scratch.

By skin type

  • Oily / acne-prone: Start with a deep cleansing facial every 2 weeks to clear congestion, then move to monthly maintenance. See our guide to the best facial for acne-prone skin.
  • Dry / dehydrated: Monthly hydrating facials, with a Hydra Resurfacing booster when your skin feels tight.
  • Normal / combination: Every 4 weeks is plenty to stay clear and glowing.
  • Mature skin: Monthly, with firming or bojin add-ons for lift.

Signs you're over-doing it

More is not better. If your skin is red, sensitised, peeling or breaking out after treatments, you're likely over-exfoliating. A good studio will space your visits out and tell you when to wait — we'd rather you came less often and saw better results.

Why "no package" matters here

Prepaid packages push you to come on their schedule, not your skin's. Because IDEAL STUDIO has no membership and no package, we book you only when a facial will help.

FAQ

Common questions

How often should I get a facial for acne?

Start with a deep cleansing facial every 2 weeks to clear congestion, then move to monthly once your skin settles.

Is it bad to get facials too often?

Yes — over-treating can sensitise skin, causing redness or breakouts. Most skin only needs a facial every 3–4 weeks.
